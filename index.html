About Mike Gonzales Sand & Gravel

At Mike Gonzales Sand & Gravel, we're more than just a business; we're a legacy of dedication and expertise. Owner/Operator Mike Gonzales has been a cornerstone in the sand and gravel industry for over 30 years, bringing unparalleled experience to every project.

Mike's extensive background covers all facets of site preparation, including building pads, driveways, excavation, and land clearing. His meticulous approach ensures the greatest structural stability, proper drainage, and rock-solid foundation support for your residential or commercial needs.

As a proud Aggie (Texas A&M University alumni) owned and operated family business, we believe in direct, personal interaction. Mike himself works closely with every client to provide tailored solutions that meet your specific project requirements and exceed expectations. When you choose us, you're choosing a partner committed to quality and integrity.

Our Expert Sand & Gravel Services

Building Pads

We construct robust and level building pads, ensuring a strong, stable foundation for homes, commercial buildings, and other structures. Proper compaction and grading are our priority.

Driveways

From initial grading to final material placement, we build durable driveways that withstand heavy traffic and harsh weather, providing excellent drainage.

Excavation

Precise excavation services for trenches, foundations, land leveling, and more. Our experienced team ensures accurate and efficient earthmoving.

Land Clearing

Prepare your land for development with our comprehensive land clearing services, removing trees, brush, and debris to create a clean slate for your project.
